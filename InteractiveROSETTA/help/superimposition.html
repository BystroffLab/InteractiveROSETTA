InteractiveROSETTA - Superimposition

Figure 1: Schematic of the superimposition panel

A: Superimposition Base Model - Specifies a fixed base model that will be the immobile structure during the superimposition. All other loaded structures will be moved to this structure.

B: Superimpose By Model - Click this button to superimpose the structures by the entire global structure.

C: Superimpose By Residues - Click this button to superimpose the structures only according to the currently selected residues.

D: Superimpose By CA - Click this button to superimpose the structures by CA atoms only.

E: Superimpose By BB - Click this button to superimpose the structures by all the backbone atoms.

F: Superimposition Results- Displays all pairwise RMSD values after a superimposition is performed.

G: Superimpose Button - Click this button to superimpose the structures with the selected settings.
